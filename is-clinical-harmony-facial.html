Pre & Post-Treatment Care

Before Your Treatment:

  • Avoid harsh exfoliants, retinol, or strong active ingredients for at least 3-5 days.
  • Avoid excessive sun exposure or tanning before treatment.
  • Stay hydrated and arrive with clean skin (no makeup, SPF, or skincare products).

After Your Treatment:

  • Apply SPF 30+ daily to protect your skin.
  • Avoid retinol, strong exfoliants, or harsh skincare products for 3-5 days.
  • Keep skin moisturized to maintain hydration and prolong results.
  • Avoid hot showers, steam rooms, or excessive heat for 24-48 hours.
  • Drink plenty of water to support skin hydration and healing.

Is the Harmony Facial painful?

No! This treatment is extremely gentle and soothing, designed specifically for sensitive or compromised skin.

How soon will I see results?

Immediately! Your skin will feel calmer, more hydrated, and refreshed after the first session.

How long do the results last?

Results typically last several weeks, and regular treatments help maintain skin comfort and hydration over time.

Can I wear makeup after the treatment?

Yes, but we recommend letting your skin breathe for at least 12 hours to maximize the benefits.

Can I combine this with other treatments?

Yes! The Harmony Facial pairs well with hydration facials, gentle LED light therapy, and barrier-repair treatments for enhanced skin restoration.
