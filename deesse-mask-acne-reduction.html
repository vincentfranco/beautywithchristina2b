Déese Mask Acne Reduction

Clearer Skin with Advanced Light Therapy

The Déese Mask Acne Reduction treatment at Beauty With Christina uses targeted light therapy to effectively treat acne and improve skin clarity. This non-invasive solution helps reduce breakouts and inflammation while promoting healthier skin.

Key Benefits:

  • Reduces acne-causing bacteria
  • Minimizes redness and inflammation
  • Improves overall skin texture
  • Non-invasive and painless treatment

Acne & Bacteria Reduction

Blue light therapy is designed to target acne-causing bacteria at its source. Operating in the 415–450nm wavelength range, blue light penetrates the skin’s surface and interacts with Propionibacterium acnes (P. acnes), a bacteria known to contribute to breakouts. By helping to neutralize bacterial activity and calm inflammation, this treatment supports clearer skin while working to minimize future blemishes.

As recommended by the manufacturer, blue light therapy may assist in the reduction of mild to moderate acne, help regulate oil production, and contribute to an overall clearer complexion. Studies also suggest that when used alongside red light therapy, blue light supports the skin’s natural healing process and may aid in reducing post-acne marks.

Redness & Inflammation Reduction

Yellow light therapy, operating at 570–590nm, is known for its calming and soothing properties. This wavelength is intended to help reduce redness, swelling, and irritation, making it particularly suitable for individuals with sensitive or reactive skin.

As recommended by the manufacturer, yellow light therapy may support lymphatic drainage, promote circulation, and assist in reducing skin irritation. It is often used post-treatment to encourage recovery and help minimize downtime following procedures such as laser treatments or chemical peels.

LED light therapy is a non-invasive and highly effective treatment that can be tailored to your specific skin concerns. Whether used as a standalone solution or combined with one of our other advanced treatments, it can help you achieve optimal skin health and rejuvenation. Book a consultation today to discuss the best approach for your skin’s unique needs.

Treatment Duration

Each DEESEE LED Light Therapy session typically lasts between 10 to 30 minutes, depending on the selected wavelength and skin concern being treated.

Standard Pre-Care Advice

To ensure optimal results, follow these guidelines before your treatment:

  • Arrive with clean, product-free skin (avoid makeup, sunscreen, or heavy skincare products).
  • Avoid active skincare ingredients (e.g., retinol, AHAs, BHAs) for at least 24 hours before treatment.
  • Stay hydrated to support skin regeneration.
  • If you have any photosensitive conditions or are using medications that increase light sensitivity, consult your therapist before booking.

Standard Post-Care Guidelines

To maintain and enhance your results, follow these post-treatment recommendations:

  • Apply broad-spectrum SPF 30+ daily to protect the skin from UV exposure.
  • Keep the skin hydrated with a gentle, non-irritating moisturizer.
  • Avoid harsh exfoliation or active skincare ingredients (e.g., retinol, strong acids) for 24 hours post-treatment.
  • If experiencing mild redness, use a soothing skincare product (e.g., aloe vera or hyaluronic acid).
  • For best results, follow the recommended treatment schedule as advised by your therapist.
