iS Clinical Cancer Care Facial

Gentle and Soothing Treatment

The Cancer Care Facial is a deeply calming, hydrating, and restorative treatment specifically designed to support delicate, stressed, or medically treated skin. Using pharmaceutical-grade botanical extracts, antioxidants, and hydration-boosting ingredients, this facial reduces redness, soothes irritation, and strengthens the skin barrier—helping to restore comfort and balance.

Originally developed to meet the unique needs of clients undergoing cancer treatments, this facial is suitable for sensitive, dry, and fragile skin that requires extra gentle care and nourishment.

FAQs - Cancer Care Facial

Key Benefits of the Cancer Care Facial

  • Soothes, calms, and hydrates dry or irritated skin
  • Strengthens the skin barrier to support healing
  • Reduces redness, sensitivity, and inflammation
  • Provides deep hydration and nourishment
  • 100% non-invasive, gentle, and safe for delicate skin
  • No downtime – just soft, comforted, and refreshed skin

Who is the Cancer Care Facial Suitable For?

  • Individuals with sensitive, dry, or compromised skin
  • Clients undergoing cancer treatments (chemotherapy, radiation, etc.)
  • Those experiencing skin stress, redness, or irritation
  • Anyone seeking a gentle, healing, and hydrating facial

This treatment is generally safe for all skin types, but please inform us of any skin conditions or health concerns before your appointment.

Pre & Post-Treatment Care

Before Your Treatment:

  • Avoid harsh exfoliants, retinol, or strong active ingredients for at least 3-5 days.
  • Avoid excessive sun exposure or tanning before treatment.
  • Stay hydrated and arrive with clean skin (no makeup, SPF, or skincare products).

After Your Treatment:

  • Apply SPF 30+ daily to protect your skin.
  • Avoid retinol, strong exfoliants, or harsh skincare products for 3-5 days.
  • Keep skin moisturized to maintain hydration and prolong results.
  • Avoid hot showers, steam rooms, or excessive heat for 24-48 hours.
  • Drink plenty of water to support skin hydration and healing.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Cancer Care Facial painful?

No! This treatment is extremely gentle and soothing, designed specifically for sensitive or compromised skin.

How soon will I see results?

Immediately! Your skin will feel calmer, more hydrated, and refreshed after the first session.

How long do the results last?

Results typically last several weeks, and regular treatments help maintain skin comfort and hydration over time.

Can I wear makeup after the treatment?

Yes, but we recommend letting your skin breathe for at least 12 hours to maximize the benefits.

Can I combine this with other treatments?

Yes! The Cancer Care Facial pairs well with hydration facials, gentle LED light therapy, and barrier-repair treatments for enhanced skin restoration.
