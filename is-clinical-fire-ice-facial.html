How Does the Fire & Ice Facial Work?

“Fire” Step – Intensive Resurfacing

Uses glycolic acid, retinol, and niacinamide to gently resurface and brighten. Boosts cell turnover and stimulates collagen production for firmer, younger-looking skin.

“Ice” Step – Deep Hydration & Soothing

A cooling mask with hyaluronic acid, aloe, and green tea extract calms and deeply hydrates the skin. Leaves the skin refreshed, soothed, and visibly plumper.

Pre & Post-Treatment Care

Before Your Treatment:

  • Avoid retinol, AHAs, BHAs, and exfoliating treatments for at least 3-5 days.
  • Avoid excessive sun exposure and tanning before treatment.
  • Stay hydrated and arrive with clean skin (no makeup, SPF, or skincare products).

After Your Treatment:

  • Apply SPF 30+ daily to protect your refreshed skin.
  • Avoid retinol, strong exfoliants, and harsh skincare for 3-5 days.
  • Keep skin moisturized to maintain hydration and prolong results.
  • Avoid saunas, steam rooms, and excessive heat for 24-48 hours.
  • Drink plenty of water to support skin hydration and cell renewal.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Fire & Ice Facial painful?

No! This treatment is completely pain-free. Some clients feel a mild tingling or warmth during the resurfacing phase, but this is temporary and tolerable.

How soon will I see results?

Instantly! Your skin will look brighter, smoother, and more hydrated immediately after the session.

How long do the results last?

Results last several weeks, and regular treatments help maintain skin clarity, hydration, and firmness over time.

Can I wear makeup after the treatment?

Yes, but we recommend letting your skin breathe for at least 12 hours to maximize the benefits.

Can I combine this with other treatments?

Absolutely! The Fire & Ice Facial pairs well with LED light therapy, hydration facials, and CACI non-surgical facelifts for even better results.
