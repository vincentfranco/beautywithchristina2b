CACI Non-Surgical Treatments

The Future of Anti-Aging & Skin Rejuvenation – No Needles, No Downtime!

CACI (Computer Aided Cosmetology Instrument) is a world-renowned brand specializing in non-surgical aesthetic treatments. Whether you’re looking to tighten facial contours, reduce wrinkles, clear acne, or improve skin hydration, CACI offers customized treatments to target your unique skincare needs.

Pre & Post-Treatment Care

Pre-Treatment Guidelines

  • Avoid excessive sun exposure or tanning 48 hours before treatment.
  • Do not apply retinol, AHAs, or strong exfoliants at least 24 hours before your session.
  • Stay hydrated and maintain a healthy skincare routine for optimal results.
  • Avoid Botox or fillers for at least two weeks before a CACI treatment.

Post-Treatment Guidelines

  • Apply SPF 30+ sunscreen daily, as the skin may be more sensitive to UV exposure.
  • Avoid harsh exfoliants, saunas, or excessive heat exposure for 24 hours.
  • Keep skin moisturized to maintain hydration and prolong results.
  • For acne treatments, avoid touching or picking the skin to prevent irritation.
  • Follow your recommended treatment plan for the best long-term results.

Who Should NOT Receive CACI Treatments?

  • ❌ Pacemakers or metal implants in the treatment area
  • ❌ Epilepsy or history of seizures
  • ❌ Active cancer or undergoing chemotherapy
  • ❌ Pregnancy (consultation required for certain treatments)
  • ❌ Recent Botox, fillers, or facial surgery (must wait at least two weeks)
  • ❌ Open wounds, active infections, or severe skin conditions

If you have any medical conditions or concerns, please consult with our specialists before booking.
