About Beauty with Christina

At Beauty with Christina, we are committed to providing high-quality, evidence-based beauty and aesthetic treatments designed to enhance your natural beauty and support your confidence at any stage of life.

Christina Franco, the "Christina" of Beauty with Christina, is a Level 3 VTCT-qualified Beauty Therapist with a specialization in anti-ageing treatments and non-surgical aesthetics. Her extensive training and expertise ensure that every client receives personalized care and the latest innovations in beauty therapy.

We believe in empowering our clients through safe, effective, and results-driven treatments. At Beauty with Christina, your comfort and satisfaction are at the heart of everything we do.

Our Services

We offer a comprehensive range of treatments tailored to your unique beauty needs. Book a no-obligation consultation today to explore options from our Body Aesthetic, Rejuvenation, and Classic Beauty Treatments collections, including:

  • CACI Non-Surgical Facelifting – Revolutionary treatments that tone, lift, and rejuvenate facial contours.
  • 3D Trilogy Ice – Laser Hair Removal – Advanced laser technology for effective, permanent hair reduction.
  • iS Clinical Fire and Ice – A clinically advanced facial that resurfaces and rejuvenates your skin.
  • iS Clinical Cancer Care – Specialized skin treatments designed with oncology patients in mind.
  • 3D Lipo Fat and Cellulite Reduction – Non-invasive solutions to sculpt and contour your body.
  • 3D Lipo Skin Tightening – Restores elasticity and firmness for a more youthful appearance.
  • Classic Beauty Treatments – Indulge in timeless therapies that leave you feeling refreshed and radiant.
