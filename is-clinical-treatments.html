FAQs - Exfoliating Clear Skin Facial

Key Benefits of the Exfoliating Clear Skin Facial

  • Deeply cleanses and purifies congested skin
  • Gently exfoliates to remove dead skin cells and excess oil
  • Unclogs pores and reduces blackheads
  • Minimizes breakouts and inflammation
  • Improves skin texture and reduces post-acne marks
  • Leaves skin feeling refreshed, smooth, and balanced

Who is the Exfoliating Clear Skin Facial Suitable For?

  • Anyone with acne-prone, oily, or congested skin
  • Individuals struggling with blackheads, whiteheads, or breakouts
  • Clients with rough, uneven skin texture
  • Those looking for a deep cleanse to maintain clear, balanced skin

This treatment is generally safe for all skin types, but please inform us of any skin conditions, allergies, or health concerns before your appointment.

How Does the Exfoliating Clear Skin Facial Work?

Gentle Chemical Exfoliation (AHA & BHA Blend)

Uses salicylic acid & glycolic acid to dissolve excess oil, unclog pores, and remove dead skin cells. Improves skin texture and helps fade post-acne marks.

Deep Pore Cleansing & Extractions

Manual extractions are performed by a professional to remove blackheads and congestion safely. Helps prevent future breakouts and reduces inflammation.

Soothing Botanical Complex

Infused with chamomile, aloe vera, and green tea to calm redness and hydrate the skin. Leaves the skin refreshed, smooth, and balanced.

Treatment time: 45-60 minutes

Results visible immediately, with long-term improvements over a course of treatments

Safe, non-irritating & suitable for acne-prone and oily skin types

Pre & Post-Treatment Care

Before Your Treatment:

  • Avoid retinol, AHAs, BHAs, and exfoliating treatments for at least 3-5 days.
  • Avoid excessive sun exposure and tanning before treatment.
  • Stay hydrated and arrive with clean skin (no makeup, SPF, or skincare products).

After Your Treatment:

  • Apply SPF 30+ daily to protect your skin.
  • Avoid retinol, strong exfoliants, and harsh skincare for 3-5 days.
  • Keep skin moisturized to prevent dryness and irritation.
  • Avoid picking or touching your skin to prevent post-treatment irritation.
  • Drink plenty of water to support skin hydration and healing.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Exfoliating Clear Skin Facial painful?

No! This treatment is designed to be effective yet gentle. Some mild tingling may occur during the exfoliation phase, but this is temporary.

How soon will I see results?

Many clients notice smoother, clearer skin immediately, with continued improvements over a course of treatments.

How long do the results last?

Results typically last several weeks, and regular treatments help prevent breakouts and maintain skin clarity.

Can I wear makeup after the treatment?

Yes, but we recommend letting your skin breathe for at least 12 hours to maximize the benefits.

Can I combine this with other treatments?

Yes! The Exfoliating Clear Skin Facial pairs well with LED light therapy, hydration facials, and CACI Acne Treatment for enhanced results.

FAQs - Prodigy Peel System

Key Benefits of the Prodigy Peel System

  • Reduces fine lines, wrinkles & uneven texture
  • Brightens skin and minimizes pigmentation & sun damage
  • Improves acne-prone skin by unclogging pores & reducing breakouts
  • Stimulates collagen for firmer, more youthful skin
  • Customizable peel strengths for different skin concerns
  • Minimal downtime compared to traditional peels

Who is the Prodigy Peel System Suitable For?

  • Individuals concerned with fine lines, wrinkles, and skin aging
  • Those looking to improve acne-prone or congested skin
  • Clients struggling with uneven skin tone, pigmentation, or sun damage
  • Anyone wanting smoother, brighter, and healthier-looking skin

This treatment is generally safe, but not recommended for highly sensitive skin or those with certain medical conditions. A consultation is required before treatment.

How Does the Prodigy Peel System Work?

Prodigy Peel "P2" (Mild-Medium Strength)

Ideal for early signs of aging, mild acne, and light pigmentation. Uses a blend of Lactic Acid, Salicylic Acid, and Resorcinol to exfoliate, brighten, and refine skin texture.

Prodigy Peel "P3" (Medium-Deep Strength)

Targets moderate to severe signs of aging, deeper pigmentation, and acne scarring. Contains a higher concentration of acids + Retinol for enhanced resurfacing, collagen stimulation, and skin renewal.

Treatment time: 30-45 minutes

Mild peeling may occur 2-5 days post-treatment, with visible improvements after 7-10 days

Professional-strength results with minimal downtime

Pre & Post-Treatment Care

Before Your Treatment:

  • Avoid retinol, AHAs, BHAs, and exfoliating treatments for at least 1 week.
  • Avoid excessive sun exposure or tanning before treatment.
  • Stop using any harsh or active skincare ingredients for at least 3-5 days.
  • Stay hydrated and arrive with clean skin (no makeup, SPF, or skincare products).

After Your Treatment:

  • Expect mild redness, dryness, and light peeling for 2-5 days.
  • Apply SPF 30+ daily to protect your healing skin.
  • Avoid retinol, strong exfoliants, and harsh skincare products for 1-2 weeks.
  • Keep skin moisturized to prevent excessive dryness and irritation.
  • Avoid picking or peeling flaking skin—let it shed naturally.
  • Avoid heat exposure (saunas, steam rooms, or intense workouts) for at least 48 hours.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Prodigy Peel painful?

You may experience a mild tingling or warmth during the peel, but it is well-tolerated. Any discomfort is temporary and subsides quickly.

How soon will I see results?

Your skin will begin to peel within 2-5 days, revealing brighter, smoother, and clearer skin by day 7-10.

How long do the results last?

Results can last several months, depending on your skin type and post-care routine. Regular treatments help maintain skin clarity, collagen production, and even tone.

Can I wear makeup after the treatment?

It’s best to avoid makeup for at least 24 hours post-peel to allow your skin to heal properly.

Can I combine this with other treatments?

Yes! The Prodigy Peel System pairs well with LED light therapy, hydration facials, and targeted serums for enhanced results.
