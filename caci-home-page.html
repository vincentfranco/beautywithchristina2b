CACI Pre- and Post-Treatment Guidance (Facial & Hand Treatments)

Pre-Care Advice

To ensure the best results, please follow these guidelines before your treatment:

  • Arrive with clean, product-free skin (no makeup, lotions, or oils on the face or hands).
  • Stay hydrated before your appointment for optimal results.
  • If you have any skin conditions, allergies, or sensitivities, inform your therapist beforehand.
  • Avoid applying self-tanner or exfoliating treatments to your face or hands 24 hours before the session.
  • Refrain from alcohol and caffeine consumption before treatment, as they can dehydrate the skin.
  • If you have open wounds, cuts, or infections on your face or hands, please reschedule your appointment.

Post-Care Guidelines

To maintain and enhance your results, follow these post-treatment recommendations:

  • Avoid direct sun exposure for at least 24 hours.
  • Apply a hydrating moisturizer or hand cream regularly to maintain results.
  • Drink plenty of water to support collagen production and skin hydration.
  • Avoid harsh skincare products, including exfoliants, acids (AHAs, BHAs, Retinol), or alcohol-based products for 24‚Äì48 hours.
  • Refrain from touching your face unnecessarily to prevent irritation.
  • Do not use hot water, steam, saunas, or intense workouts that cause excessive sweating for at least 24 hours.
  • If redness or sensitivity occurs, apply a gentle, fragrance-free moisturizer.
  • For best results, follow the recommended course of treatments and use CACI-approved aftercare products.
