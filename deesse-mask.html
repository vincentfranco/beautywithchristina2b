Déesse Mask Treatments

Déesse Mask LED Light Therapy offers a range of specialized treatments designed to rejuvenate, repair, and enhance your skin. Each session utilizes targeted wavelengths of light to address specific skin concerns, ensuring a tailored experience for every client.

  • Anti-Aging
  • Acne Reduction
  • Skin Brightening
  • Skin Healing
  • Relaxation

Anti-Aging

Stimulates collagen production to firm the skin and reduce fine lines and wrinkles, leaving you with a more youthful complexion.

  • Light Used: Red Light (630nm–650nm)
  • Duration: 20 minutes
  • Pre-Treatment Care: Avoid retinol or exfoliating products 48 hours prior.
  • Post-Treatment Care: Apply SPF and a hydrating serum.

Acne Reduction

Eliminates acne-causing bacteria and prevents future breakouts, resulting in clearer and healthier skin.

  • Light Used: Blue Light (405nm–420nm)
  • Duration: 20 minutes
  • Pre-Treatment Care: Avoid heavy makeup or pore-clogging products.
  • Post-Treatment Care: Use a gentle moisturizer and acne-specific products.
Learn More

Skin Brightening

Fades dark spots and evens out skin tone for a luminous, radiant complexion.

  • Light Used: Green Light (520nm–540nm)
  • Duration: 20 minutes
  • Pre-Treatment Care: Avoid chemical exfoliants 48 hours before the session.
  • Post-Treatment Care: Apply SPF and brightening serums.
Learn More

Skin Healing

Accelerates recovery from inflammation and irritation, restoring skin health and balance.

  • Light Used: Near-Infrared Light (800nm–900nm)
  • Duration: 25 minutes
  • Pre-Treatment Care: Cleanse skin gently before the session.
  • Post-Treatment Care: Use calming products to support recovery.
Learn More

Relaxation

A soothing, non-invasive treatment that promotes relaxation and stress relief while rejuvenating the skin.

  • Light Used: Yellow Light (570nm–590nm)
  • Duration: 30 minutes
  • Pre-Treatment Care: Arrive with clean skin, free of makeup.
  • Post-Treatment Care: Apply moisturizer and enjoy the calming effects.
