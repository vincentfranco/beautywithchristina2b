Pre & Post-Treatment Care

Before Your Treatment:

  • Avoid retinol, AHAs, BHAs, and exfoliating treatments for at least 3-5 days.
  • Avoid excessive sun exposure or tanning before treatment.
  • Stay hydrated and arrive with clean skin (no makeup, SPF, or skincare products).

After Your Treatment:

  • Apply SPF 30+ daily to protect your refreshed skin.
  • Avoid retinol, strong exfoliants, and harsh skincare for 3-5 days.
  • Keep skin moisturized to maintain hydration and prolong results.
  • Avoid saunas, steam rooms, and excessive heat for 24-48 hours.
  • Drink plenty of water to support skin hydration and cell renewal.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Foaming Enzyme Facial painful?

Not at all! This treatment is gentle and relaxing, with a light tingling sensation during the foaming phase.

How soon will I see results?

Immediately! Your skin will look brighter, smoother, and refreshed right after the session.

How long do the results last?

Results typically last several weeks, and regular treatments help maintain skin clarity, hydration, and brightness over time.

Can I wear makeup after the treatment?

Yes, but we recommend letting your skin breathe for at least 12 hours to maximize the benefits.

Can I combine this with other treatments?

Absolutely! The Foaming Enzyme Facial works beautifully with LED light therapy, hydration facials, and Fire & Ice Facials for enhanced results.
