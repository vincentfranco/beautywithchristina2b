The Déese Mask Relaxation treatment at Beauty With Christina offers a soothing and rejuvenating experience, combining advanced LED light therapy with a tranquil environment to promote relaxation. Key Benefits:

  • Helps reduce skin sensitivity and redness
  • Provides a calming, spa-like experience
  • Non-invasive and soothing treatment
  • EMay help reduce stress and promote overall well-being

Calming & Soothing Sensitive Skin

Cyan light therapy, in the 490–510nm range, is designed to support skin that is prone to irritation or stress-related breakouts. It is commonly used to help reduce redness, promote hydration, and enhance overall skin balance.

The manufacturer recommends cyan light therapy for those with sensitive or reactive skin, as it may assist in strengthening the skin’s natural barrier while promoting a calm, refreshed appearance. Additionally, it is often used alongside other LED treatments to help maintain comfort and minimize potential skin stress.

LED light therapy is a non-invasive and highly effective treatment that can be tailored to your specific skin concerns. Whether used as a standalone solution or combined with one of our other advanced treatments, it can help you achieve optimal skin health and rejuvenation. Book a consultation today to discuss the best approach for your skin’s unique needs.

Treatment Duration

Each DEESEE LED Light Therapy session typically lasts between 10 to 30 minutes, depending on the selected wavelength and skin concern being treated.

Standard Pre-Care Advice

To ensure optimal results, follow these guidelines before your treatment:

  • Arrive with clean, product-free skin (avoid makeup, sunscreen, or heavy skincare products).
  • Avoid active skincare ingredients (e.g., retinol, AHAs, BHAs) for at least 24 hours before treatment.
  • Stay hydrated to support skin regeneration.
  • If you have any photosensitive conditions or are using medications that increase light sensitivity, consult your therapist before booking.

Standard Post-Care Guidelines

To maintain and enhance your results, follow these post-treatment recommendations:

  • Apply broad-spectrum SPF 30+ daily to protect the skin from UV exposure.
  • Keep the skin hydrated with a gentle, non-irritating moisturizer.
  • Avoid harsh exfoliation or active skincare ingredients (e.g., retinol, strong acids) for 24 hours post-treatment.
  • If experiencing mild redness, use a soothing skincare product (e.g., aloe vera or hyaluronic acid).
  • For best results, follow the recommended treatment schedule as advised by your therapist.
